# Special Origins

### 🩸 Vampire
The creature of the night.
- **Pros**:
  - **Unholy Reach**: +1 Reach distance.
  - **Blood Suck**: Shift + Right-Click to drain health (Costs Mana).
  - **Blood Feast**: Regeneration I after feeding.
- **Cons**:
  - **Sun Allergy**: Massive burn damage in daylight.
  - **Blood Hunger**: Only heal via Blood Suck (No natural regeneration).

---

### 🐺 Werewolf
The moon-bound shifter.
- **Pros**:
  - **Moon Mutation**: Massive buffs at night and full moons (Scale, HP, Damage).
  - **Primal Instinct**: Huge unarmed damage in wolf form.
- **Cons**:
  - **Silver Sensitivity**: Double damage from Iron weapons.

---

### 🧜‍♀️ Siren
The master of the depths.
- **Pros**:
  - **Aquatic**: Breathe underwater, swim 50% faster.
  - **Heat Steal**: Shift + Right-Click for a frost aura (Costs Mana).
- **Cons**:
  - **Dehydration**: Wither on land if dry.
  - **Reverse Oxygen**: Drown on land.

---

### 🧚 Fae-Touched
Light and magical.
- **Pros**:
  - **Wings**: Double Jump ability.
  - **Nature's Mend**: Constant natural regeneration.
- **Cons**:
  - **Iron Weight**: Heavy armor reduces speed.
