# Monster Origins

### 🧟 Undead
A walking corpse.
- **Pros**:
  - **Necrotic Body**: Undead are neutral.
  - **Rotten Feast**: Eating Rotten Flesh heals you if full.
- **Cons**:
  - **Sunbeam Allergy**: Burn in daylight.
  - **Holy Weakness**: Healing potions damage you.

---

### 👹 Orc
A massive powerhouse.
- **Pros**:
  - **Colossus**: 1.3x Scale.
  - **Brutal Strength**: +15% Melee damage.
  - **Thick Hide**: -20% Damage taken.
- **Cons**:
  - **Dull Mind**: -10% Wisdom.

---

### 🐲 Dragonblood
Kin of the ancient dragons.
- **Pros**:
  - **Fireborn**: Immune to fire/lava.
  - **Fire Breath**: Shift + Right-Click to breathe fire (Costs Mana).
- **Cons**:
  - **Hydrophobia**: Taking damage in water/rain.

---

### 🧨 Creeper
Volatile and dangerous.
- **Pros**:
  - **Volatile**: Shift + Right-Click to prime an explosion (3 stages).
  - **Immunity**: Immune to own explosion.
- **Cons**:
  - **Feline Phobia**: Weakness near Feline players.

---

### 🐺 Wolf
The loyal hunter.
- **Pros**:
  - **Pack Tactics**: +2% Damage per nearby tamed wolf.
  - **Alpha Howl**: Shift + Right-Click to heal pack (Costs Mana).
- **Cons**:
  - **Meat Eater**: Can only eat meat.
