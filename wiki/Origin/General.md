# General Origins

### 🌾 Human
The jack of all trades.
- **Pros**:
  - **Jack of all Trades**: +0.1% XP Gain per Wisdom point.
- **Cons**:
  - **Nocturnal Fatigue**: -10% Attack/Speed & -15% XP at night.

---

### 🐱 Feline
Agile and stealthy.
- **Pros**:
  - **Cat-like Reflexes**: Reduced fall damage.
  - **Scary**: Creepers flee from you.
  - **Stealth**: Hostile mobs have 50% reduced detection range.
  - **Night Hunter**: Random gifts when waking up.
- **Cons**:
  - **Small**: 0.5x Scale.
  - **Fragile**: -20% Max Health.

---

### ⛏️ Dwarf
Master of the depths.
- **Pros**:
  - **Miner**: Permanent Haste I while mining.
  - **Underground Sight**: Night Vision in caves/night.
- **Cons**:
  - **Dense**: Sinks in water.
  - **Sunlight Sensitivity**: Blindness in sun without helmet.

---

### 🏹 Wood Elf
Nature's archer.
- **Pros**:
  - **Nature's Grace**: Night Vision in forests.
  - **Focus**: +20% damage on charged bow shots.
- **Cons**:
  - **Fragile**: -10% Max Health.

---

### 👺 Goblin
Greedy and nimble.
- **Pros**:
  - **Greedy**: 40% Villager discount with Gold.
  - **Nimble**: +25% Speed.
- **Cons**:
  - **Weak**: -30% Damage.
