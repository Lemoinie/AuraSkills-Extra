# Rare Spells (Tier 3)

### ⚡ Arcane Surge
- **Description**: A short dash-blink forward.
- **Bonus**: Reduces the mana cost of your next spell by 10%.
- **Mana Cost**: High.

### ⛈️ Storm Calling
- **Description**: Harness the power of the sky to change the weather.
- **Effect**: Sets the world weather to a Thunderstorm for 1 hour.
- **Aesthetics**: Cloud and lightning particles around the caster.
- **Mana Cost**: 150.
- **Cooldown**: 300s.
