# Mythic Spells (Tier 5)

### 🏮 Malevolent Shrine
- **Description**: A devastating ritual that slashes every enemy in a 50-block radius.
- **Slashes**: 300 slashes per enemy.
- **Duration**: 30 seconds (10 slashes per second).
- **Optimization**: Automatically stops slashing dead enemies.
- **Mana Cost**: 300.
- **Cooldown**: 120s.
