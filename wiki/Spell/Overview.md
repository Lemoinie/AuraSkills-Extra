## Mechanics
- **Mana**: Every spell consumes mana provided by AuraSkills.
- **Cooldowns**: Spells have individual cooldowns displayed in the action bar.
- **Learning**: Spells are learned via the `/ske spells` menu using XP and Wisdom.
