# Legendary Spells (Tier 4)

### ☄️ Meteor
- **Description**: Summons a massive meteor from the sky.
- **Explosion**: Radius 6.0 (Non-destructive).
- **Mana Cost**: Very High.
- **Cooldown**: 45s.

### ⛈️ Thunderstorm
- **Description**: Strikes all nearby enemies with lightning multiple times.
- **Synergy**: Deals **20% more damage** during a Thunderstorm.
- **Mana Cost**: Very High.
- **Cooldown**: 30s.

### ☄️ Meteor Shower
- **Description**: Rains down 12 mini-meteors in a radius.
- **Mana Cost**: Very High.
- **Cooldown**: 60s.

### ⚡ Chain Lightning
- **Description**: A lightning beam that jumps between up to 5 enemies.
- **Synergy**: Deals **20% more damage** during a Thunderstorm.
- **Mana Cost**: Very High.
- **Cooldown**: 20s.
