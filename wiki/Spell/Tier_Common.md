# Common Spells (Tier 1)

### 💨 Kinetic Shove
- **Description**: A short-range wind blast that knocks mobs back.
- **Mana Cost**: Base.
- **Scaling**: Knockback scales with **Strength**.

### 🔥 Spark
- **Description**: Shoots a tiny fire projectile.
- **Effect**: Ignites players and TNT.
- **Mana Cost**: Low.

### 💡 Mage Light
- **Description**: Summons a floating light orb.
- **Duration**: 60 seconds.
- **Mana Cost**: Low.
