# Uncommon Spells (Tier 2)

### 🌿 Nature's Root
- **Description**: Freezes a single mob in place.
- **Duration**: 3 seconds.
- **Mana Cost**: Medium.

### ☄️ Firebolt
- **Description**: A fast fire projectile.
- **Damage**: Moderate + Burn.
- **Mana Cost**: Medium.

### ❄️ Frost Touch
- **Description**: Slows an enemy by 40%.
- **Duration**: 4 seconds.
- **Mana Cost**: Medium.
