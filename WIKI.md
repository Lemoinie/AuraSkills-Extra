# AuraSkills-Extra Wiki: Origins

Welcome to the official Wiki for AuraSkills-Extra Origins. Here you will find everything you need to know about the different species available.

---

## 🌾 Human
The jack of all trades.
- **Pros**:
  - **Jack of all Trades**: +0.1% Experience Gain from all AuraSkills sources for every point of **Wisdom** you possess.
- **Cons**:
  - **Nocturnal Fatigue**: Being a creature of the day, you grow tired at night. Receives **-10% Attack Damage**, **-10% Movement Speed**, and **-15% Experience Gain** when the sun goes down (`/time set 12000`).

---

## 🐱 Feline
Agile, nimble, and misunderstood.
- **Pros**:
  - **Cat-like Reflexes**: Take 20% less fall damage. Cancels damage entirely when sneaking.
  - **Scary**: Creepers are terrified of you and will flee on sight.
  - **Stealth**: Hostile mobs have a **50% reduced detection range** for you, making you much harder to find.
  - **Night Hunter**: Just like a cat, you might find a gift by your bed when you wake up!
  - **Agile**: +10% XP Gain from the **Agility** skill.
- **Cons**:
  - **Small**: Reduced size (0.5x scale) makes you harder to hit but more fragile.
  - **Fragile**: -20% Max Health.
  - **Nine Lives**: Slightly lower attack power (-15%).

---

## ⛏️ Dwarf
Masters of the underground.
- **Pros**:
  - **Miner**: Permanent Haste I while mining blocks.
  - **Underground Sight**: Night Vision automatically active when in caves or at night.
  - **Master Miner**: +10% XP Gain from the **Mining** skill.
- **Cons**:
  - **Dense**: Your heavy bones cause you to sink rapidly in water.
  - **Sunlight Sensitivity**: Exposure to direct sunlight causes Blindness unless wearing a helmet.
  - **Undergrounder**: Villagers find your presence unsettling, increasing trade costs by 10%.
  - **Slow**: Your stout build reduces movement speed by 10%.

---

## 🏹 Wood Elf
Graceful archers of the forest.
- **Pros**:
  - **Nature's Grace**: Permanent Night Vision when in forest biomes.
  - **Focus**: Hold a bow for 2 seconds to trigger a **Focused Shot**, dealing 20% more damage.
  - **Archer**: +10% XP Gain from the **Archery** skill.
- **Cons**:
  - **Fragile**: -10% Max Health.

---

## 👺 Goblin
Greedy, fast, and opportunistic.
- **Pros**:
  - **Greedy**: While wearing at least one piece of Golden Armor (or holding a Golden tool), you receive a **40% discount** on all Villager trades.
  - **Nimble**: You have a permanent **+25% Movement Speed** bonus.
  - **Scamper**: You gain an additional **+5% Speed** for every living entity (mob or player) within a 5-block radius (Max 50% bonus).
- **Cons**:
  - **Small**: -30% Max Health.
  - **Weak**: -30% Attack Damage.

---

## 🧟 Undead
A walking corpse, bound by unholy magic.
- **Pros**:
  - **Necrotic Body**: Zombies, Skeletons, Phantoms, and other undead are neutral to you.
  - **Rotten Feast**: Rotten Flesh provides **8 Food** and **10 Saturation**. If your food bar is full, eating it will **heal you for 2.0 (1 heart)**.
  - **Flesh Eater**: Hitting living entities heals you for a small portion of the damage dealt.
  - **Unholy Vitality**: Harming and Poison potions heal you instead of dealing damage.
- **Cons**:
  - **Sunbeam Allergy**: You catch fire in direct sunlight unless wearing a helmet.
  - **Outcast**: Villagers are terrified and will refuse to trade with you.
  - **Holy Weakness**: Healing potions and regeneration effects damage you.

---

## 👹 Orc
A massive warrior of unparalleled strength.
- **Pros**:
  - **Colossus**: You stand tall at 1.3x normal human scale.
  - **Brutal Strength**: Baseline +15% Melee damage.
  - **Thick Hide**: -20% Damage taken from all sources.
  - **Warrior**: +10% XP Gain from **Fighting** and **Defense** skills.
- **Cons**:
  - **Intimidating**: Your presence causes Villagers to charge 50% higher prices.
  - **Dull Mind**: -10% Wisdom (Global Experience gain reduction).

---

## 🐲 Dragonblood
Cousin to the rulers of the End and the Nether.
- **Pros**:
  - **Fireborn**: Complete immunity to Fire and Lava.
  - **Blazing Strikes**: Fire attacks (including Fire Breath) deal **+35% damage** if you hold a Fire Aspect weapon.
  - **Fire Breath**: Hold **Shift + Right-Click** to breathe a 3-block wide cone of fire. Deals 4.0 (up to 5.4 with Blazing Strikes) damage per 5 ticks and costs Mana.
- **Cons**:
  - **Aquatic Weakness**: Water slows you down significantly (Slowness I).
  - **Hydrophobia**: Taking **1 HP damage every 2 seconds** when in water or rain.

---

## 🧚 Fae-Touched
Etheral, light, and intrinsically magical.
- **Pros**:
  - **Wings**: Ability to **Double Jump**. Just tap jump twice while in the air!
  - **Nocturnal**: Move 15% faster at night (Speed II).
  - **Nature's Mend**: Constant natural health regeneration.
  - **Scholar**: +10% XP Gain from the **Enchanting** skill.
- **Cons**:
  - **Iron Weight**: Your magical nature is weighed down by heavy metals. Heavy armor (Iron, Diamond, Netherite) significantly reduces your speed.
  - **Fragile**: -10% Max Health.

---

## 🩸 Vampire
- **Pros**:
  - **Immortal Soul**: Permanent Health Regeneration I.
  - **Blood Suck**: Shift + Right-Click a living entity with an empty hand to drain their life. Deals **2.0 True Damage**, heals you for 1.0, and **Costs 5 Mana**. Has a **1 second cooldown**.
  - **Nocturnal**: You deal 20% more damage at night, but only 50% damage during the day.
  - **Beetroot Allergy**: Receives Weakness II if any player within 5 blocks has beetroot.
  - **Fragile**: -20% maximum health.
- **Cons**:
  - **Sun Allergy**: Take massive damage in direct sunlight.
  - **Weakened State**: Deals -50% damage during the day.

---

## 🐺 Werewolf
- **Pros**:
  - **Moon Mutation**: Transform based on time and moon phase.
    - **Day**: Scale 1.0, -50% HP, -30% Damage.
    - **Night**: Scale 1.5, +30% HP, +10% Damage.
    - **Full Moon**: Scale 2.0, +50% HP, +20% Damage.
  - **Primal Instinct**: Massive unarmed combat bonuses (+70% to +100% damage).
- **Cons**:
  - **Silver Sensitivity**: Receives double damage from Iron weapons.
  - **Iron Armor**: Iron armor burns your skin.

---

## 🧜‍♀️ Siren
An aquatic humanoid with a voice like the sea and a body forged in the depths.

- **Pros**:
  - **Aquatic**: You can breathe underwater, see clearly in the deep, and swim **50% faster**.
  - **Ocean's Gift**: Permanent Night Vision while underwater.
  - **Heat Steal**: Hold **Shift + Right-Click** to project a frost aura (3-block radius). Nearby entities continuously freeze and take **1.0 damage** per 10 ticks. **Costs 4 Mana/second**.
  - **Wave Dash**: When in water, **double-click Space** to dash forward or upward.
- **Cons**:
  - **Dehydration**: You must stay in water or rain. If you stay on land too long, you will begin to wither.
  - **Nether Weakness**: You cannot survive in the Nether; you will constantly be on fire.
  - **Reverse Oxygen**: You breathe water but drown on land. Your oxygen will **not regenerate** while on land.
  - **Land Slowness**: You are unaccustomed to land travel, resulting in permanent **Slowness III** while out of water.
  - **Fragile Grip**: Your hands are designed for water, making you unable to use shields.

---

## 🧨 Creeper
A volatile creature of the shadows, ready to go out with a bang.
- **Pros**:
  - **Volatile Ability**: Hold **Shift + Right-Click** to prime yourself. Release to trigger the explosion!
    - **No Block Damage**: The explosion does not destroy blocks.
    - **Multi-Stage Power**:
      - **Stage 1 (<5s)**: Normal explosion (Base Damage). No extra cost. Feed: Smoke.
      - **Stage 2 (5s-10s)**: Mana-scaled explosion. Consumes all Mana. **+1% damage per Mana point**. Feedback: Flame.
      - **Stage 3 (>10s)**: Suicide explosion. Consumes all Mana and all HP (Suicide). **+1% per Mana AND +5% per current HP point**. Feedback: Soul Fire.
    - **Immunity**: You are immune to your own explosion damage.
  - **Explosive XP**: +10% XP Gain from all combat skills.
- **Cons**:
  - **Feline Phobia**: Receives **Weakness II** when within 8 blocks of a **Feline** origin player.
  - **Fragile**: -20% Max Health.
  - **Small**: Reduced size (0.8x scale).

---

## 🐺 Wolf
The loyal companion of the wild, now walking among men.
- **Pros**:
  - **Skeleton Scaring**: Skeletons, Bogged, and Strays are terrified of you and will not attack unless provoked.
  - **Pack Tactics**: Deal **+2% damage** for every tamed wolf within 10 blocks.
  - **Alpha Howl**: Hold **Shift + Right-Click** to grant **Regeneration II** to all your tamed wolves within 10 blocks. **Costs 10 Mana per wolf**.
  - **Tamed Buffs**: Your tamed wolves are stronger, possessing **+30% Max Health** and **+10% Attack Damage**.
  - **Wild Kinship**: Wild wolves are passive towards you even if you strike them.
- **Cons**:
  - **Small**: Reduced size (0.75x scale).
  - **Fragile**: -15% Max Health.

## ✨ New Feature: Rebirth Ritual
Switch origins at any time by performing the ritual:
1. Have the **Weakness** effect active.
2. Stand on a **Block of Diamond**.
3. Consume an **Enchanted Golden Apple**.

**Action**: Your current origin will be instantly removed, and the origin selection menu will open.
