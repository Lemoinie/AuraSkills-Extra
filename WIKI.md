# AuraSkills-Extra Wiki: Origins

Welcome to the official Wiki for AuraSkills-Extra Origins. Here you will find everything you need to know about the different species available.

---

## 🌾 Human
The jack of all trades.
- **Pros**:
  - **Jack of all Trades**: +0.1% Experience Gain from all AuraSkills sources for every point of **Wisdom** you possess.
- **Cons**:
  - **Nocturnal Fatigue**: Being a creature of the day, you grow tired at night. Receives **-10% Attack Damage**, **-10% Movement Speed**, and **-30% Experience Gain** when the sun goes down (`/time set 12000`).

---

## 🐱 Feline
Agile, nimble, and misunderstood.
- **Pros**:
  - **Cat-like Reflexes**: Take 20% less fall damage. Cancels damage entirely when sneaking.
  - **Scary**: Creepers are terrified of you and will flee on sight.
  - **Stealth**: Hostile mobs have a **50% reduced detection range** for you, making you much harder to find.
  - **Night Hunter**: Just like a cat, you might find a gift by your bed when you wake up!
  - **Agile**: +10% XP Gain from the **Agility** skill.
- **Cons**:
  - **Small**: Reduced size (0.5x scale) makes you harder to hit but more fragile.
  - **Fragile**: -20% Max Health.
  - **Nine Lives**: Slightly lower attack power (-15%).

---

## ⛏️ Dwarf
Masters of the underground.
- **Pros**:
  - **Miner**: Permanent Haste I while mining blocks.
  - **Underground Sight**: Night Vision automatically active when in caves or at night.
  - **Master Miner**: +10% XP Gain from the **Mining** skill.
- **Cons**:
  - **Dense**: Your heavy bones cause you to sink rapidly in water.
  - **Sunlight Sensitivity**: Exposure to direct sunlight causes Blindness unless wearing a helmet.
  - **Undergrounder**: Villagers find your presence unsettling, increasing trade costs by 10%.
  - **Slow**: Your stout build reduces movement speed by 10%.

---

## 🏹 Wood Elf
Graceful archers of the forest.
- **Pros**:
  - **Nature's Grace**: Permanent Night Vision when in forest biomes.
  - **Focus**: Hold a bow for 2 seconds to trigger a **Focused Shot**, dealing 20% more damage.
  - **Archer**: +10% XP Gain from the **Archery** skill.
- **Cons**:
  - **Fragile**: -10% Max Health.

---

## 👺 Goblin
Greedy, fast, and opportunistic.
- **Pros**:
  - **Greedy**: Gain a 40% discount on all Villager trades while wearing or holding gold.
  - **Nimble**: +25% Movement Speed baseline.
- **Cons**:
  - **Small**: -30% Max Health.
  - **Weak**: -30% Attack Damage.

---

## 🧟 Undead
A walking corpse, bound by unholy magic.
- **Pros**:
  - **Soul Bound**: Undead mobs (Zombies, Skeletons, Phantoms, etc.) are neutral towards you.
  - **Grave Digestion**: Rotten flesh provides 8 food and 10 saturation, and gives a Strength buff without the hunger penalty.
  - **Unholy Vitality**: Harming and Poison potions heal you instead of dealing damage.
- **Cons**:
  - **Sunbeam Allergy**: You catch fire in direct sunlight unless wearing a helmet.
  - **Outcast**: Villagers are terrified and will refuse to trade with you.
  - **Holy Weakness**: Healing potions and regeneration effects damage you.

---

## 👹 Orc
A massive warrior of unparalleled strength.
- **Pros**:
  - **Colossus**: You stand tall at 1.3x normal human scale.
  - **Brutal Strength**: Baseline +15% Melee damage.
  - **Thick Hide**: -20% Damage taken from all sources.
  - **Warrior**: +10% XP Gain from **Fighting** and **Defense** skills.
- **Cons**:
  - **Intimidating**: Your presence causes Villagers to charge 50% higher prices.
  - **Dull Mind**: -10% Wisdom (Global Experience gain reduction).

---

## 🐲 Dragonblood
Cousin to the rulers of the End and the Nether.
- **Pros**:
  - **Fireborn**: Complete immunity to Fire, Fire Tick, and Lava damage.
  - **Blazing Strikes**: Fire-based attacks (Fire Aspect/Flame) deal +35% total damage.
- **Cons**:
  - **Aquatic Weakness**: Water slows you down significantly (Slowness I).
  - **Hydrophobia**: Being wet (in water or rain) reduces your total damage output by 10%.

---

## 🧚 Fae-Touched
Etheral, light, and intrinsically magical.
- **Pros**:
  - **Wings**: Ability to **Double Jump**. Just tap jump twice while in the air!
  - **Nocturnal**: Move 15% faster at night (Speed II).
  - **Nature's Mend**: Constant natural health regeneration.
  - **Scholar**: +10% XP Gain from the **Enchanting** skill.
- **Cons**:
  - **Iron Weight**: Your magical nature is weighed down by heavy metals. Heavy armor (Iron, Diamond, Netherite) significantly reduces your speed.
  - **Fragile**: -10% Max Health.

---

## 🩸 Vampire
- **Pros**:
  - **Bloodlust**: Deals +20% damage at night.
  - **Immortal Soul**: Permanent Health Regeneration I.
  - **Blood Drinker**: Shift + Right-Click with an empty hand on a living mob or player to heal.
- **Cons**:
  - **Sun Allergy**: Take massive damage in direct sunlight.
  - **Weakened State**: Deals -50% damage during the day.
  - **Beetroot Allergy**: Receives Weakness II if any player within 5 blocks has beetroot.
  - **Fragile**: -20% maximum health.

---

## 🐺 Werewolf
- **Pros**:
  - **Moon Mutation**: Transform based on time and moon phase.
    - **Day**: Scale 1.0, -50% HP, -30% Damage.
    - **Night**: Scale 1.5, +30% HP, +10% Damage.
    - **Full Moon**: Scale 2.0, +50% HP, +20% Damage.
  - **Primal Instinct**: Massive unarmed combat bonuses (+70% to +100% damage).
- **Cons**:
  - **Silver Sensitivity**: Receives double damage from Iron weapons.
  - **Iron Armor**: Iron armor burns your skin.

---

## ✨ New Feature: Rebirth Ritual
Switch origins at any time by performing the ritual:
1. Have the **Weakness** effect active.
2. Stand on a **Block of Diamond**.
3. Consume an **Enchanted Golden Apple**.

**Action**: Your current origin will be instantly removed, and the origin selection menu will open.
