# AuraSkills-Extra: Origins

A powerful expansion for AuraSkills that introduces a flexible **Origins** system. Choose your path, embrace your strengths, and survive your weaknesses.

## Features
- **Origin Selection**: Pick an origin upon first join or anytime via `/ske origins`.
- **Unique Perks**: Each origin comes with active and passive abilities, custom scaling, and attribute modifiers.
- **Specialized XP**: Origins now have specialized experience gain multipliers for specific AuraSkills.
- **Rebirth Ritual**: Reset your origin at any time by consuming an Enchanted Golden Apple while standing on a Diamond Block under the effect of Weakness.
- **Deep Integration**: Seamlessly integrates with AuraSkills menus and attribute system.
- **Persistence**: Your choices are saved across server restarts.

## Origins at a Glance
- **Human**: The balanced choice. +0.1% XP bonus per Wisdom. Tired at night.
- **Feline**: Small and stealthy. Hostile mobs detect you from half range.
- **Dwarf**: The master miner. Haste and Night Vision. +10% XP from Mining.
- **Wood Elf**: Bow specialist. +10% XP from Archery.
- **Goblin**: Small but very fast. 40% discounts with gold.
- **Siren**: Master of the sea. Breathes in water but drowns on land. Vulnerable to heat.
- **Undead**: Neutral to other undead. Rotten flesh is your fuel.
- **Orc**: Massive size and melee power. +10% XP from Fighting & Defense.
- **Dragonblood**: Fire immune and blazing strikes.
- **Fae-Touched**: Wings and nocturnal power. +10% XP from Enchanting.
- **Vampire**: Blood drinker, high night damage, massive sun weakness.
- **Werewolf**: Moon-based mutations, unarmed combat master, iron weakness.

## Requirements
- **Paper 1.21.1+**
- **AuraSkills 2.x**
